Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Puzzlora Puzzle – Brain Game ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation, and we do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

The following non-personal data is collected automatically to make the App function:

DataPurpose
Anonymous Device IDUsed to save your puzzle progress, points, and leaderboard ranking. This ID does not identify you personally.
Game progress & pointsStored to track completed puzzles, unlocked levels, and your leaderboard score.
App usage analyticsBasic, anonymized usage statistics (e.g. screens viewed, puzzles completed) collected via Firebase Analytics to help us improve the App.
Advertising identifiersCollected by Google AdMob to show and measure ads, as described below.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Analytics) – used to store game progress, leaderboard data, and app usage analytics.
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob – used to display banner, interstitial, and rewarded video advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.
    https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Advertising

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob to support free access to the App. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads, and to measure ad performance. You can control ad personalization through your device's settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience, including children, and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Ads shown within the App are served in compliance with Google Play's Families Policy where applicable. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below and we will take steps to remove it.

5. Data Storage & Security

Game progress and leaderboard data are stored securely using Google Firebase. We take reasonable measures to protect this data, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Deletion

Since the App does not collect personally identifiable information, there is no account to delete. If you wish to have your anonymous device data (game progress/leaderboard entry) removed from our servers, please contact us with your in-app Device ID (visible on the Home screen) and we will delete it upon request.

By using the App, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
